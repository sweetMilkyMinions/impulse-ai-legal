# Privacy Policy for Impulse AI Last updated: May 5, 2026 Impulse AI ("we", "our", "the app") helps users pause before impulsive decisions around money, food, and emotions. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it. ## 1. Information we collect - **Account info**: name, age, gender, email — provided by you during onboarding or via Google Sign-In. - **Profile data**: avatar choice, focus areas (money / body / emotion), income, fixed/variable expenses, weight, fitness goal, calorie target, emotional triggers. - **Usage data**: the decisions you log ("about to do") and the AI responses generated for them, used to power your Insights dashboard. - **Device data**: standard diagnostic info (OS version, crash logs) via Google Play services. ## 2. How we use it - Personalize AI responses to your situation. - Power dashboards (money saved, calories avoided, happy-day streaks). - Improve reliability and fix crashes. We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** show third-party ads. ## 3. Where data is stored Your profile and decision history are stored securely on Supabase (EU/US regions). AI responses are generated via the Rork AI gateway. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. ## 4. Sharing We share data only with: - **Supabase** — database hosting. - **Google Sign-In** — authentication. - **Rork AI gateway** — to generate AI responses (prompts only, no identifying info attached). We never share your data for advertising. ## 5. Your rights You can: - Edit your profile, avatar, income, weight, and triggers in Settings. - Sign out at any time. - Request full deletion of your account and data by emailing us. ## 6. Children Impulse AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. ## 7. Changes If we update this policy, we'll change the "Last updated" date and, for material changes, notify you in-app. ## 8. Contact Questions, deletion requests, or concerns: **sweetmilkyminions@gmail.com**
