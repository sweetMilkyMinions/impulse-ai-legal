Privacy Policy

Impulse AI · Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Impulse AI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using Impulse AI, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: name, age, gender, and email address (auto-filled when you sign in with Google).
  • Profile preferences: chosen avatar and focus areas (money, body, emotion).
  • Goal data you provide: monthly income, expenses, weight, fitness targets, emotional triggers — only what you choose to share.
  • Usage data: impulses logged, decisions made, and counters (money saved, calories avoided, mood streaks).
  • Device data: basic anonymous diagnostics for crash reporting and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize AI responses based on your profile and chosen focus areas.
  • To display your progress on the Insights dashboard.
  • To improve app stability and user experience.
  • To send relevant notifications (only if you opt in).

3. AI Processing

When you describe an impulse, your input may be sent securely to a third-party AI provider to generate a structured response. We do not sell or share this content. Inputs are processed transiently and not used to train external models.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard providers (Supabase / Google Cloud). Sensitive fields are encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with:

  • Authentication providers (Google) for sign-in.
  • AI processing providers strictly to generate your responses.
  • Legal authorities, only when required by law.

6. Subscriptions & Payments

Impulse AI may offer optional in-app subscriptions through the Google Play Store. Payment data is handled entirely by Google — we never see or store your card details.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, edit, or delete your profile data anytime from Settings.
  • Request full account deletion by emailing us (see contact below).
  • Withdraw consent for any optional data collection.

8. Children's Privacy

Impulse AI is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated personal data is erased within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
